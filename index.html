With proper care, most of our fresh bouquets last 7–10 days. Trim the stems at an angle every 2–3 days, keep them in fresh water, and place them away from direct sunlight and heat to maximise their lifespan.

Keep your bouquet in a clean vase with fresh, cool water. Re-trim the stems diagonally every 2 days, remove any leaves below the waterline to prevent bacteria, and change the water daily if possible. Keep flowers away from direct sunlight, heat vents, and bowls of fruit.
